The Taste of the Wonders

In the early 1980s, a group of artists - founded by Wild Turner - invaded the streets of Saint- Paul, a charming village nestled on the north shore of the Saint-Laurent River. The group is named Wild Circus - among which there is a couple of acrobats Cécile & Roman, a diabolist Nicolas Pires, and a contortionist Babou Flex - walks on stilts, juggling, dancing, spits fire and plays music; intriguing and marveling residents of the place.




Cécile & Roman

Cécile & Roman A couple of aerial acrobats, on stage and in life, Cécile & Roman worked with Kamel Ouali on the musical "Dracula" With their show they moved to tears the jury of the show: «La France a un incroyable talent».

These aerial acrobats present "instant de vie", a piece of their work with aerial straps. With them, you will share a moment of magic and poetry.




Nicolas Pires

Nicolas Pires Le Castelroussin King the diabolo goes into high gear. He has crossed the world, giving a demonstration in HK, where he worked at Disneyland, also in Brazil and Canada, where he lay down his hat, but not his dragged.

The past two years he lived in Châteauroux, the city where he was born almost thirty years ago, its home port. Nicolas Pires, with his diabolo organizes internship during the school holidays and take the opportunity to create «le spectacle Diabolique» Sa return to basis for this globetrotter, who just come back from NY, where he worked on his show, with Christophe, the Director the the Conservatory.




Babou Flex

Babou Flex, Contortionist/Dancer from Guinée, is a rubber man.

He went to the TV show “Le plus grand cabaret du Monde” with Patrick Sebastien in June 2011. He has worked with major circuses: Cirque Mandingue, Festival du cirque in Cambodia He also created, on a proposal of Vinc Lajge, an enchanting choreography, twisting his body to suit his desires. He presents «l’arbre».
